OUR STORY

Why Vietnam?

In 2007, John visited Ho Chi Minh city - Vietnam for the first time and was impressed by local cuisine and hospitable people.

Where in Vietnam?

Street food, defenitely! It is the cheapest and one of the most delicious places to find cuisine in Vietnam.

After the trip

John decided to strart a Vietnamese restaurant in Canada where people could enjoy the same taste as they were in Vietnam.

Since then

Lavn has been one of the most favorite restaurant for not only Vietnamese but also the foreigners.
